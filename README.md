# 🎓 AI Placement & Internship Assistant

An AI-powered web application designed to help engineering students find the best internships and placements based on their skills, with personalized guidance and learning roadmaps.

## 🌟 Key Features

### 📊 Resume Score
Analyzes your skills and gives a score out of 100 with suggestions to improve your profile.

### 💼 Internship Role Matcher
Matches your skills with internship roles like:
- Data Science Intern
- ML Engineer Intern
- Web Developer Intern
- Data Analyst Intern
- Python Developer Intern
- AI Research Intern

### 🏢 Company Matcher
Shows top companies hiring for each role including TCS, Zoho, Infosys, Wipro, Deloitte, Google, and more.

### 🎤 Interview Question Generator
Generates role-specific interview questions based on your skills to help you prepare for internship interviews.

### 🗺 30-Day Learning Roadmap
Provides a personalized week-by-week learning plan to help you acquire missing skills and land your dream internship.

### 🔗 Direct Apply Links
Quick links to top internship platforms:
- Internshala
- LinkedIn Jobs
- Forage Virtual Internships
- Naukri
- Indeed India
